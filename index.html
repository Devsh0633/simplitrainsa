Common Questions

What Saudi training teams usually ask us.

Is SimpliTrain available in Arabic?+
Yes. The platform, learner portals, and certificates are available in Arabic and English, along with other languages, so your team and your learners can each work in the language they're most comfortable with.
Can SimpliTrain run training across multiple Saudi cities at once?+
Yes. Multi-location and multi-tenant operations are core to the platform, not an add-on. Training companies run several branches, and enterprises coordinate training across multiple sites, all from one system.
We already use another LMS or TMS. Do we have to replace it?+
No. SimpliTrain is modular. Many customers run it purely as the ILT/vILT operations layer for scheduling, trainer management, and attendance on top of an existing LMS like Cornerstone, Docebo, or SAP SuccessFactors. You can consolidate onto SimpliTrain's native LMS later, on your own timeline, or not at all.
Where is our training and learner data hosted?+
Data residency and hosting requirements vary by organization, especially for government and enterprise buyers. Talk to our team about your specific requirements and we'll walk you through what applies to your setup under Saudi Arabia's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL).
How does SimpliTrain help with Nitaqat 2026 and profession-specific Saudization audits?+
Nitaqat's 2026 overhaul requires profession-specific accreditations and digitally documented training records across 269 roles. SimpliTrain provides real-time, audit-ready attendance logs, skill tracking, and automatic certificate issuance mapped by location and profession, ensuring your compliance records pass Qiwa, HRDF, and MHRSD verification without manual paperwork.
Can SimpliTrain track training metrics for Saudi Aramco's IKTVA scoring and contractor certifications (WPR / CWQAP)?+
Yes. For oil & gas suppliers and third-party institutes, SimpliTrain automates recurring certification cycles (Work Permit Receiver, H2S awareness, permit-to-work) and exports verified, audit-ready training spend and completion data that directly feeds into your third-party audited IKTVA local-content score for Aramco contract renewals.
Does SimpliTrain work alongside HRDF (Hadaf) and Doroob-subsidized training programs?+
Yes. HRDF subsidies increase learner volume, which often overwhelms spreadsheet-based coordination. SimpliTrain manages the operational scheduling, attendance, and evaluation data required for HRDF reimbursement reporting, while our flat pricing model ensures your software costs remain unchanged no matter how much your subsidized learner volume grows.
How does the platform support regulatory compliance for healthcare (SCFHS CME), aviation (GACA), and TVTC centers?+
SimpliTrain tracks mandatory attendance percentages (such as SCFHS's 75% threshold for CME credits), logs instructor allocation for TVTC multi-branch centers, and maintains immutable inspection records for GACA audits. Certification is automatically triggered only when all regulatory compliance criteria are satisfied.
How long does implementation take?+
Using SimpliTrain as a TMS layer over an existing LMS typically takes 4–6 weeks to go live. A full-platform implementation covering TMS, LMS, assessments, and multi-location operations typically takes 8–12 weeks, including data migration and trainer onboarding.
How does SimpliTrain's pricing work for professional training companies?+
For professional training companies, SimpliTrain offers predictable flat-rate pricing based on the number of administrators and instructors running training, rather than charging per learner. For enterprise requirements or a custom quote tailored to your organization, contact SimpliTrain.
