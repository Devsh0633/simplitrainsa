Common Questions

What Saudi training teams usually ask us.

Is SimpliTrain available in Arabic?+
Yes. The platform, learner portals, and certificates are available in Arabic and English, along with other languages, so your team and your learners can each work in the language they're most comfortable with.
Can SimpliTrain run training across multiple Saudi cities at once?+
Yes — multi-location and multi-tenant operations are core to the platform, not an add-on. Training companies run several branches, and enterprises coordinate training across multiple sites, all from one system.
We already use another LMS or TMS. Do we have to replace it?+
No. SimpliTrain is modular. Many customers run it purely as the ILT/vILT operations layer — scheduling, trainer management, attendance — on top of an existing LMS like Cornerstone, Docebo, or SAP SuccessFactors. You can consolidate onto SimpliTrain's native LMS later, on your own timeline, or not at all.
Where is our training and learner data hosted?+
Data residency and hosting requirements vary by organization, especially for government and enterprise buyers. Talk to our team about your specific requirements and we'll walk you through what applies to your setup.
How long does implementation take?+
Using SimpliTrain as a TMS layer over an existing LMS typically takes 4–6 weeks to go live. A full-platform implementation — TMS, LMS, assessments, and multi-location operations — typically takes 8–12 weeks, including data migration and trainer onboarding.
Is pricing really the same regardless of how many learners we train?+
Yes. SimpliTrain's flat pricing is based on the number of administrators and instructors running training, not the number of learners. A program with 500 learners and one with 50,000 cost the same to run, provided the team size stays the same.
